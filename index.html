About Me

Rooted in practice,
guided by heart

I'm Shikha — a certified yoga teacher and lifelong student of the practice. My journey began during a challenging chapter of my life, and yoga became the anchor that helped me find clarity, strength, and deep inner peace.

I believe yoga is not a performance, but a conversation between your body, breath, and mind. My classes are welcoming, unhurried, and crafted to meet you exactly where you are — whether you're stepping onto the mat for the first time or deepening a decades-long practice.

My teaching blends Hatha foundations with Vinyasa flow and Yin stillness, always returning to the breath as the truest teacher.

Yin Yoga Certified, Insight Yoga Institute
